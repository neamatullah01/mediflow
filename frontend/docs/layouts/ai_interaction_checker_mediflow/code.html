Major Interaction

Warfarin + Simvastatin

Moderate Interaction

Explanation: Simvastatin may enhance the anticoagulant effect of warfarin by inhibiting its metabolism or through unknown mechanisms, potentially increasing INR.

Clinical Recommendation:
